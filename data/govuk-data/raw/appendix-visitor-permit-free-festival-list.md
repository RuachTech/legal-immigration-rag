Immigration Rules Appendix Visitor: Permit Free Festival List
=============================================================

List of Permit Free Festivals

1.   PFF1. An artist, entertainer or musician visiting the UK to perform at one or more of the following permit free festivals may receive payment to do so: 
    1.   (a) Aldeburgh Festival
    2.   (b) All Points East
    3.   (c) American Express presents BST Hyde Park
    4.   (d) Barbican Festivals – Live from the Barbican
    5.   (e) Belfast International Arts Festival
    6.   (f) Belsonic
    7.   (g) Big Church Festival
    8.   (h) Billingham International Folklore Festival of World Dance
    9.   (i) Birmingham Jazz & Blues Festival
    10.   (j) Boomtown Festival
    11.   (k) Bradford Literature Festival
    12.   (l) Breakin’ Convention
    13.   (m) Brighton Festival
    14.   (n) Brighton Fringe
    15.   (o) Camp Bestival Dorset
    16.   (p) Celtic Connections
    17.   (q) Cheltenham Festivals (Jazz, Science, Music & Literature Festivals)
    18.   (r) Creamfields
    19.   (s) C2C: Country to Country Festival
    20.   (t) DaDaFest International
    21.   (u) Dance Umbrella
    22.   (v) Download
    23.   (w) Durham BRASS Festival
    24.   (x) Edinburgh Festival Fringe
    25.   (y) Edinburgh International Book Festival
    26.   (z) Edinburgh International Children’s Festival
    27.   (aa) Edinburgh International Festival
    28.   (bb) Edinburgh International Jazz and Blues Festival
    29.   (cc) Emerge Belfast
    30.   (dd) Flamenco Festival at Sadler’s Wells Theatre
    31.   (ee) Freedom Festival Arts Trust
    32.   (ff) Garsington Opera
    33.   (gg) Glasgow International Jazz Festival
    34.   (hh) Glastonbury Festival
    35.   (ii) Global Streets
    36.   (jj) Glyndebourne
    37.   (kk) Greenbelt
    38.   (ll) Greenwich and Docklands International Festival
    39.   (mm) Green Man
    40.   (nn) Harrogate International Festivals
    41.   (oo) Hay Festival Foundation Ltd
    42.   (pp) Huddersfield Contemporary Music Festival
    43.   (qq) Hyde Park Winter Wonderland
    44.   (rr) Isle of Wight Festival
    45.   (ss) Latitude
    46.   (tt) Leeds Festival
    47.   (uu) Llangollen International Musical Eisteddfod
    48.   (vv) Love Supreme
    49.   (ww) Manchester International Festival
    50.   (xx) Meltdown (Southbank Centre)
    51.   (yy) Milton Keynes International Festival
    52.   (zz) Norfolk & Norwich Festival
    53.   (aaa) Out There Festival
    54.   (bbb) Parklife
    55.   (ccc) Reading Festival
    56.   (ddd) Shubbak
    57.   (eee) Southbank Centre Festivals & Series
    58.   (fff) Summer at Snape
    59.   (ggg) Take Me Somewhere Festival
    60.   (hhh) Terminal V
    61.   (iii) The EFG London Jazz Festival
