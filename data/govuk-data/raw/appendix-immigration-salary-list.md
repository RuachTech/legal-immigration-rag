Title: Immigration Rules - Immigration Rules Appendix Immigration Salary List - Guidance

URL Source: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/immigration-rules/immigration-rules-appendix-immigration-salary-list

Markdown Content:

An occupation is only included on the list where an application has been made using a certificate of sponsorship issued by a sponsor to an applicant before the removal date stated in the table.
